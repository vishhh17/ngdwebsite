Why do we exist?

While during surgery, we are always one mishap away from a disaster, so it's always better to march into battle of life and death well prepared.

To avoid the surprises in the surgery, we provide you with visual/physical anatomical models for surgical purposes. Performing a mock surgery on the precise model prepared from the CT and MRI scans of the patient avoids any surprises in surgery and also boosts the morale of the surgeon to whom you have trusted your life. This increases the chances of success by at least 11%.

About our Services

So, what do these complex terminologies mean?

3D Printed Pre-Surgical Models:

Get a 3D printed Model for the Patient's case according to the necessity of the Surgery/Operation from their CT and MRI scans. A real 3D Printed model (using FDM/SLS/SLA 3D Printing Technology) for Preoperative and Operative guidance and reference, along with a Virtual 3D model in various file formats for further applications if necessary.

3D Printed Surgical Guides:

Another manner in which 3D Printing can be useful during surgeries is the development of Personalized Surgical Guides for the assistance of the surgeon to reduce the chances of surgical errors to the minimum and help them attain optimum accuracy in the absence of surprise elements. Get 3D Printed surgical guides (using FDM/SLS/SLA 3D Printing Technology) for the surgery.

DICOM Images to CAD Model:

Get a Virtual 3D Model generated for the patient’s case from their CT and MRI scans in real time. It helps in Visualization of the patient’s case in real time for preoperative surgical planning as it can be exported as 3D digital models in various file formats that can be used by numerous applications. It also improves comfort and efficiency of your Doctor/Surgeon to comprehend and operate.
