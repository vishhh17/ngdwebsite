Welcome to NextGen Diastasi.

We provide Pre-Operative Solutions and Consultancy to doctors across India in terms of surgical assistance via Computer Aided Designing from DICOM Images (CT and MRI scans) and 3D Printing.

DICOM to CAD Model

Get a Virtual 3D Model generated for the patient’s case from their CT and MRI scans in real time.

DICOM to CAD Model with Haptic Device

Get a Virtual 3D Model generated for the patient’s along with a Haptic Device demostration for your Doctor's reference in real time.

3D Printed Pre-surgical models

Get a 3D printed Model for the Patient's case according to the necessity of the Surgery/Operation from their CT and MRI scans.

3D Printed surgical guides

Get 3D Printed surgical guides (using FDM/SLS/SLA 3D Printing Technology) for the surgery.

Consultation / Solutions

Confused on which service would be most helpful regarding your Patient’s case? Contact us.
